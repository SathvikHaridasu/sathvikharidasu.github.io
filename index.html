BayMax AI Health Assistant

Baymax Bot is an innovative health assistant designed to make healthcare accessible, efficient, and user-friendly. Inspired by the compassionate and helpful Baymax from Big Hero 6, this AI-powered chatbot combines advanced technologies to provide quick and reliable injury assessments. With its camera integration, Baymax Bot can analyze images of injuries, identify the type of injury, and offer tailored first-aid advice or next steps. Whether it’s a minor cut, bruise, or something more serious, Baymax Bot delivers helpful insights right at your fingertips. Built with a focus on simplicity and approachability, Baymax Bot ensures that users of all ages can access medical guidance with ease. From addressing immediate concerns to logging symptoms for future consultations, Baymax Bot is your friendly, pocket-sized healthcare companion.

HarmoniQ

HarmoniQ is an intelligent facial recognition program designed to understand and respond to human emotions in real time. By using advanced tools like OpenCV for video processing, FER for emotion detection, and DeepFace for in-depth facial analysis, HarmoniQ offers seamless and accurate recognition of expressions such as happiness, sadness, anger, and more. This innovative software captures and analyzes facial cues to execute customized commands based on detected emotions, making it ideal for applications in customer service, mental health support, and personal assistance. Its user-friendly design ensures accessibility while showcasing the power of AI-driven emotional intelligence. Developed as part of a high school team project for the Neo Developer League, HarmoniQ combines creativity and technical expertise. The project earned 4th place at the NeoDev hackathon, demonstrating its potential to enhance human-computer interactions through emotion-based responses.

Naloxone App

NaloxNow is an innovative mobile application designed to combat Canada’s opioid crisis by providing real-time access to naloxone kits, emergency navigation, and AI-driven overdose response guidance. Leveraging cutting-edge technologies like Google Maps API, AI chatbots, and cloud-based workflows, the app empowers communities to save lives during overdose emergencies. Users can locate the nearest naloxone kits, upload new kit locations, and receive step-by-step instructions during crises. With features like automated 911 calls, emergency contact alerts, and live GPS tracking, NaloxNow bridges the gap between technology and public health, creating a scalable, community-driven solution to one of Canada’s most urgent challenges. This project showcases my passion for engineering impactful solutions that address real-world problems and improve lives.
